### 2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

**2.1 Definition.** "**Confidential Information**" means any ~~non-public~~ information disclosed by ~~one Party (the "Disclosing Party") to the other Party (the "Receiving Party")~~ **[INITECH ADD: Initech to Vendor]**, whether disclosed orally, in writing, or by any other means, ~~that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and the circumstances of disclosure~~ **[INITECH ADD: regardless of whether it is marked or identified as confidential at the time of disclosure]**. Confidential Information includes, without limitation, business plans, financial information, technical data, product roadmaps, pricing, customer lists, and trade secrets **[INITECH ADD: , regulatory and compliance documentation, internal policies and procedures, information relating to Initech's clients and counterparties, and any other information relating to Initech's business, operations, or strategic initiatives, including information disclosed verbally in any meeting, call, or demonstration]**.

*[INITECH COMMENT: The "reasonably should be understood" standard is too ambiguous and creates disputes. Everything we share should be covered, full stop. We operate in a regulated environment and cannot have gaps in what is protected.]*

**2.2 Exclusions.** Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party; (b) was rightfully known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure ~~without restriction~~; (c) is rightfully received by the Receiving Party from a third party ~~without restriction~~ **[INITECH ADD: provided Vendor can demonstrate such third party was authorized to make such disclosure]**; or (d) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of ~~the Disclosing Party's~~ **[INITECH ADD: Initech's]** Confidential Information **[INITECH ADD: , provided Vendor maintains written records sufficient to demonstrate such independent development]**.

*[INITECH COMMENT: We have tightened the exclusions. "Without restriction" is vague. Vendor bears the burden of proving any exclusion applies.]*

### 3. OBLIGATIONS

**3.1 Non-Disclosure.** ~~Each Receiving Party~~ **[INITECH ADD: Vendor]** agrees to: (a) hold ~~the Disclosing Party's~~ **[INITECH ADD: Initech's]** Confidential Information in strict confidence using at least the same degree of care it uses to protect its own confidential information, but no less than ~~reasonable~~ **[INITECH ADD: industry-leading]** care; (b) not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of ~~the Disclosing Party~~ **[INITECH ADD: Initech]**; and (c) use the Confidential Information solely for the Purpose.

**3.2 Permitted Disclosures.** ~~A Receiving Party~~ **[INITECH ADD: Vendor]** may disclose Confidential Information to its employees~~, officers, directors, advisors, and contractors~~ **[INITECH ADD: and full-time employees only]** ("**Representatives**") who: (a) have a need to know such information for the Purpose; and (b) are bound by written confidentiality obligations at least as protective as those in this Agreement **[INITECH ADD: , copies of which shall be provided to Initech upon request]**. ~~Each Party is~~ **[INITECH ADD: Vendor is]** responsible for any breach of this Agreement by its Representatives.

*[INITECH COMMENT: Contractors and advisors are not acceptable. We need to know exactly who touches our information. Full-time employees only.]*

**3.3 Required Disclosures.** If ~~a Receiving Party~~ **[INITECH ADD: Vendor]** is required by law, regulation, or court order to disclose Confidential Information, it will: (a) promptly notify ~~the Disclosing Party~~ **[INITECH ADD: Initech]** in writing to the extent permitted by law; (b) reasonably cooperate with ~~the Disclosing Party's~~ **[INITECH ADD: Initech's]** efforts to seek a protective order or other appropriate relief; and (c) disclose only the minimum amount of information required. **[INITECH ADD: Vendor shall bear all costs associated with seeking such protective relief.]**
