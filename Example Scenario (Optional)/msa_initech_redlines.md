### 2. SERVICES

**2.1 Provision of Services.** Vendor will make the Services available to Client in accordance with this Agreement and the applicable Order Form. Vendor will use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure the Services are available ~~99.5%~~ **[INITECH ADD: 99.9%]** of the time in any given calendar month, excluding scheduled maintenance windows communicated at least ~~48 hours~~ **[INITECH ADD: ten (10) business days]** in advance. **[INITECH ADD: Scheduled maintenance may not occur during business hours (Monday to Friday, 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM Eastern Time).]**

*[INITECH COMMENT: 99.5% equates to approximately 3.6 hours of downtime per month. That is not acceptable for a platform handling financial documents. 99.9% is our minimum standard for any SaaS vendor. Maintenance windows must be outside business hours.]*

**2.2 Professional Services.** No change.

**2.3 Changes to Services.** Vendor may update or modify the Services from time to time, provided that no update will materially reduce the core functionality available to Client ~~during a paid subscription term without at least ninety (90) days' prior written notice~~ **[INITECH ADD: . Any modification to the Services requires Client's prior written approval if it could affect Client's regulatory compliance obligations or materially alter the user experience. Vendor shall provide a written impact assessment at least ninety (90) days prior to any such change.]**

*[INITECH COMMENT: As a regulated financial institution, we cannot have a vendor unilaterally modify a platform we rely on for contract review. We need approval rights over material changes.]*

**2.4 Support.** Vendor will provide ~~standard technical support during business hours (Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM Eastern Time), excluding Canadian statutory holidays~~ **[INITECH ADD: technical support on a 24/7/365 basis with the following response time commitments: (a) Critical issues (platform unavailable or data loss risk): response within one (1) hour, resolution within four (4) hours; (b) High priority issues (material feature unavailable): response within two (2) hours, resolution within eight (8) hours; (c) Standard issues: response within one (1) business day]**. Enhanced support terms may be specified in an Order Form.

*[INITECH COMMENT: Business hours support is not acceptable. Our teams work across time zones and we need 24/7 coverage. This is standard for any enterprise SaaS contract at Initech.]*

### 4. FEES AND PAYMENT

**4.1 Fees.** No change.

**4.2 Payment Terms.** Invoices are due within ~~thirty (30)~~ **[INITECH ADD: sixty (60)]** days of the invoice date. ~~Overdue amounts bear interest at 1.5% per month (18% per annum).~~ **[INITECH ADD: Overdue amounts bear interest at the rate of 0.5% per month.]**

*[INITECH COMMENT: Net 60 is our standard AP cycle. We will not commit to Net 30. The 18% interest rate is punitive and unacceptable.]*

**4.3 Taxes.** No change.

**4.4 Fee Increases.** Vendor may increase fees upon renewal by no more than ~~five percent (5%)~~ **[INITECH ADD: three percent (3%)]** over the prior term's fees, provided Vendor gives Client at least ~~sixty (60)~~ **[INITECH ADD: ninety (90)]** days' written notice before the renewal date.

**4.5 Disputed Invoices.** If Client disputes any portion of an invoice in good faith, Client will: (a) ~~pay the undisputed portion by the due date~~ **[INITECH ADD: have the right to withhold payment of the full invoice until the dispute is resolved]**; (b) provide written notice of the disputed amount and the basis for the dispute within ~~fifteen (15)~~ **[INITECH ADD: thirty (30)]** days of the invoice date; and (c) cooperate with Vendor to resolve the dispute promptly. **[INITECH ADD: Vendor shall not suspend Services during the pendency of any good faith dispute.]**

*[INITECH COMMENT: We cannot be obligated to pay an invoice we are disputing. Withholding payment during a dispute is standard. Vendor also cannot hold our access hostage while a billing dispute is being resolved.]*

### 5. TERM AND TERMINATION

**5.1 Term.** No change.

**5.2 Termination for Cause.** Either party may terminate this Agreement or any Order Form upon written notice if the other party: (a) materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within ~~thirty (30)~~ **[INITECH ADD: fifteen (15)]** days of written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail; or (b) becomes insolvent, makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors, or has a receiver appointed. **[INITECH ADD: 5.2(c) Client may terminate this Agreement or any Order Form for convenience upon thirty (30) days' written notice to Vendor, without liability for any fees beyond the current billing period.]**

*[INITECH COMMENT: 30 days to cure is too long for a material breach. 15 days is sufficient. We also require a convenience termination right — we cannot be locked into a vendor relationship that is not working for us.]*

**5.3 Effect of Termination.** Upon termination: (a) Client's right to access the Services ceases immediately **[INITECH ADD: , unless Client terminates for cause, in which case access continues for thirty (30) days to facilitate transition]**; (b) ~~all outstanding fees become immediately due and payable~~ **[INITECH ADD: fees are due only for Services received up to the termination date; no early termination fees apply]**; (c) Vendor will make Client Data available for export for ~~thirty (30) days~~ **[INITECH ADD: ninety (90) days]**, after which Vendor may delete Client Data; and (d) each party will return or destroy the other party's Confidential Information upon written request.

**5.4 Survival.** No change.

### 7. LIABILITY

**7.1 Limitation of Liability.** To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, ~~each party's~~ **[INITECH ADD: Vendor's]** total aggregate liability to ~~the other party~~ **[INITECH ADD: Client]** arising out of or related to this Agreement, whether in contract, tort, or otherwise, shall not exceed the total fees paid or payable by Client in the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim. **[INITECH ADD: Notwithstanding the foregoing, the liability cap in this Section 7.1 shall not apply to: (a) claims arising from a breach of Section 9 (Data Protection) or any unauthorized access to or disclosure of Client Data; (b) Vendor's gross negligence or wilful misconduct; or (c) Vendor's indemnification obligations under Section 7.4. In the case of (a), Vendor's liability shall be uncapped.]**

*[INITECH COMMENT: A liability cap on data breaches is unacceptable for a financial institution. We are subject to OSFI oversight and the potential regulatory and reputational exposure from a data breach far exceeds 12 months of fees. The cap can stay for ordinary commercial disputes but not for data incidents.]*

**7.2 Exclusion of Consequential Damages.** ~~Neither party shall be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, punitive, or consequential damages, including lost profits or loss of data, even if advised of the possibility of such damages.~~ **[INITECH ADD: Vendor shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, or punitive damages. The exclusion of consequential damages shall not apply to Client's claims arising from a data breach, unauthorized disclosure of Confidential Information, or Vendor's gross negligence or wilful misconduct.]**

*[INITECH COMMENT: We cannot waive our right to consequential damages in the event of a data breach. The consequential damage exclusion is acceptable for routine commercial disputes but not for data incidents.]*

**[INITECH ADD: 7.3 Minimum Insurance. Vendor shall maintain throughout the term: (a) commercial general liability insurance of not less than $5,000,000 per occurrence; (b) professional liability (errors and omissions) insurance of not less than $5,000,000 per occurrence; and (c) cyber liability insurance of not less than $10,000,000 per occurrence. Vendor shall provide certificates of insurance to Client upon request.]**

**[INITECH ADD: 7.4 Indemnification. Vendor shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Client and its officers, directors, employees, and affiliates from and against any claims, damages, losses, and expenses (including reasonable legal fees) arising from: (a) Vendor's breach of this Agreement; (b) any claim that the Services infringe a third party's intellectual property rights; or (c) any unauthorized access to or disclosure of Client Data caused by Vendor's acts or omissions.]**

*[INITECH COMMENT: We require a full indemnification provision and minimum insurance coverage. These are standard requirements for all Initech technology vendors.]*

### 9. DATA PROTECTION

**9.1 Data Processing.** To the extent Vendor processes personal information on behalf of Client, Vendor will: (a) process such data only in accordance with Client's documented instructions; (b) implement ~~appropriate~~ **[INITECH ADD: industry-leading]** technical and organizational security measures ~~;~~ **[INITECH ADD: consistent with ISO 27001 or SOC 2 Type II standards, evidence of which shall be provided to Client annually;]** and (c) notify Client ~~promptly~~ **[INITECH ADD: within twenty-four (24) hours]** upon becoming aware of any unauthorized access to Client Data.

*[INITECH COMMENT: "Appropriate" is subjective and unenforceable. We require ISO 27001 or SOC 2 Type II certification. The 24-hour breach notification window is required under our OSFI obligations.]*

**9.2 Data Location.** Client Data will be stored and processed in Canada **[INITECH ADD: , specifically within the Province of Ontario,]** unless otherwise agreed in writing. **[INITECH ADD: Vendor shall not transfer, replicate, or back up Client Data outside of Ontario without Client's prior written consent.]**

**[INITECH ADD: 9.3 Audit Rights. Upon reasonable prior written notice of not less than forty-eight (48) hours, Client shall have the right, no more than twice per calendar year, to audit Vendor's information security controls, data handling practices, and compliance with this Agreement, either directly or through a qualified third-party auditor. Vendor shall cooperate fully with any such audit and bear the costs of its own personnel in connection therewith.]**

*[INITECH COMMENT: Audit rights are mandatory under our OSFI third-party risk management requirements. 48 hours notice is reasonable. We have removed the "once per year" limitation given the sensitivity of the data involved.]*

**9.~~3~~4 Compliance.** No change.
